    How I became interested in cars

    When I look back on my childhood, I always picture 3 things. Learning how to ride a motorcycle, my little grey powerwheels truck, and my little wooden radio flyer bike. Those were all key parts in my childhood. Especially the truck. My dad helped me build 2 wooden trailers with strap points, and I would load up my bike, tow it around, then unload it and ride it, as if I was going on a real motorcycle trip. As I got older, I got better at riding motorcycles, and driving cars. By the time I actually got my permit, I had already driven cars alot, and knew exactly how to handle them. These were key points in my early life that made me interested in what I am today.
